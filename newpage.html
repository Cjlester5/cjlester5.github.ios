Cultivate Your Garden of Possibilities

Lilies: There are multiple types of lilies, over 2000 different types! With numerous categories to be classified, it can lead to many choices.


Sunflowers: There are 70 species of sunflowers! The term sunflower comes from the technical term Helianthus. Helios meaning Sun, and Anthos meaning flower.


Orchids: In the entire world, there is a range of 25,000-30,000 different species of Orchids! It is also known that at least 10,000 of them can be found in the tropics.
